A Man

Should know what he is,
And what he can be,
And what he should be.

people don't always take advantage of their powers.
Everyone should know what he is. and what he can be.

And if one takes advantage of the power of what he could be -
he will be happy and it is good for the whole world.

God will help that everyone will really know what he is, and he will take advantage of all the forces he has for heaven sake.
